# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing an inclusive and welcoming community for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

Be respectful and considerate. Harassment and unwelcome behavior are not acceptable.

## Responsibilities and Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content that violates these standards. Report inappropriate behavior to _t a b k r a m [at] g m a i l [dot] c o m_. Violations will be reviewed and may result in corrective action.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces and in public spaces when representing the project.
